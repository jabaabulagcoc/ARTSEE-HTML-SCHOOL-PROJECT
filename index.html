About Us

Artsee Cafe: Where Coffee Meets Creativity

At Artsee Cafe, we believe that art isn’t just something you hang on a wall — it’s part of everyday life. Founded in 2024, we started with a simple idea: to create a space where great coffee and creative expression go hand in hand.

Our name, "Artsee," is a play on words — it’s about seeing art in the little things, being seen by the community around you, and looking at the world with fresh eyes. Whether you’re an artist looking for a workspace, a coffee lover seeking a cozy corner, or someone who just wants to explore local talent, you belong here.

We serve carefully sourced coffee and homemade treats, but our menu extends beyond food and drink. Our walls rotate with works from emerging local artists, we host weekly art workshops, and we provide a platform for creators to showcase and sell their craft. For us, supporting artists isn’t just an add-on — it’s at the heart of what we do.

Come for the coffee, stay for the inspiration. We can’t wait to welcome you.
